Our Story

Elevate
Your Journey

Founded in 2012, Atlas Aviation was born from a simple belief: that the journey should be as exceptional as the destination. Our founders — seasoned aviators and luxury travel veterans — set out to create a charter service with no equal.

We serve discerning travelers aged 30–60+, C-suite executives, and luxury enthusiasts who demand exclusive experiences, premium efficiency, and absolute privacy. We solve real pain points: rigid schedules, crowded airports, and the loss of precious time.

Today, Atlas Aviation operates a world-class fleet with a global concierge network — delivering bespoke air travel to every corner of the globe.
